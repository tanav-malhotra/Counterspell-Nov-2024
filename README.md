# Counterspell-Nov-2024
## Theme: 
> You are your own enemy.
## Game Idea:
> A game where you are running from your own shadow that copies your exact moves and timing. The shadow gets quicker as you progress through the game. You can move either up or down and levels are infinite/randomized. Your score is dependent on how far you get from the starting position (vertically).
